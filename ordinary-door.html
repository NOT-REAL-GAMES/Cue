An Ordinary Door

ACT ONE

INT. KITCHEN - MORNING

A kettle screams on a spotless stove. MARA, 30s, watches the back door like it owes her money.

MARA

(under her breath)

Not today.

CLOSE ON: THE BACK DOOR

The handle turns by itself.

MARA

Tell me you locked that.

JON

I locked that.

The door should feel mundane until this exact moment.
